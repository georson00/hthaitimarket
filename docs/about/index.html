About HT Haiti Market

Connecting Haitians around the world with trusted products and businesses in Haiti.

Our Mission

HT Haiti Market was created to help Haitians living abroad support their families and communities back home. Our goal is to make it easier to purchase everyday products, construction materials, school supplies, household essentials, and other important items for loved ones in Haiti.

Through technology, we hope to strengthen the connection between the Haitian diaspora and Haiti while supporting local businesses.

Why We Exist

Millions of Haitians living outside of Haiti regularly send financial support to family members. However, money alone does not always guarantee access to quality products when they are needed.

HT Haiti Market aims to provide a more direct solution by allowing customers to purchase products from trusted Haitian businesses and suppliers through a simple online marketplace.

What We Offer

Food & Groceries

Essential food products for Haitian families, including rice, beans, sugar, pasta, and everyday household necessities.

Construction Supplies

Building materials such as cement, paint, doors, windows, and other products needed for construction and home improvement.

Electrical Supplies

Solar panels, batteries, generators, inverters, and other products that help provide reliable energy solutions.

Household & Appliances

Furniture, appliances, and household products that improve daily living and comfort.

Personal Care

Personal hygiene and wellness products such as toothpaste, soap, lotion, and other essential care items.

School & Office Supplies

Educational and office products including backpacks, notebooks, printers, pens, and learning materials.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Haitian businesses can easily reach customers around the world while providing families in Haiti with convenient access to quality products and services.

By supporting local businesses and encouraging economic activity, HT Haiti Market hopes to contribute to Haiti's long-term development and prosperity.

Founder

HT Haiti Market was founded by Nelson Georges, a Haitian software development student and entrepreneur who believes technology can help strengthen connections between Haitians living abroad and their families back home.

This project combines technology, entrepreneurship, and a desire to contribute to Haiti's future by creating opportunities for local businesses and easier access to products for Haitian families.
